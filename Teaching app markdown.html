Unlike the stand-alone melting simulators, this app is structured as a **guided curriculum** with: - üß© **Four curated learning modes** - üéì Integrated **tutorial panels** - üìù **Quick-check quizzes** inside every mode - üìä Real-time melting curves, derivative plots, Tm estimation, and strain-matching tools - üî¨ Embedded ‚ÄúFoundations‚Äù notes explaining concepts as students interact This app delivers a complete, hands-on understanding of melting physics and its applications in **PCR analysis, variant detection, viral identification, and synthetic data generation**. --- # üîß 2. Glossary **Tm (Melting Temperature)** ‚Äì temperature at which 50% of DNA is melted **Fraction Melted** ‚Äì proportion of dsDNA converted to ssDNA at a given temperature **Derivative (dF/dT)** ‚Äì rate of melting; peak indicates Tm **GC%** ‚Äì proportion of G+C bases; increases duplex stability **RMSD** ‚Äì root-mean-square deviation between two curves **Cluster** ‚Äì group of viral strains with similar melting behavior --- # üèÅ 8. Closing Notes The **DNA Melting Teaching App** is a complete interactive system for understanding melting physics from first principles to advanced diagnostic applications. Across its four modes, students build intuition for: - how sequence composition shapes stability - how to compare reference and variant amplicons - how melting curves can identify viral strains - how to generate synthetic data for scientific workflows In conclusion, the DNA Melting Teaching App brings everything together in a way that makes learning melting behavior a lot more intuitive.
